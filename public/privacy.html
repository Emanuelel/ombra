Privacy Policy

Last updated 11 July 2026

Ombra (“Ombra”, “we”, “us”) is a location-based game for finding shaded bar and restaurant terraces in Barcelona, checking in, and competing for the “crown” of a terrace on a weekly leaderboard. Ombra is operated by Selene App Studio. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, and your rights over it.

1. Data we collect

  • Google account — when you sign in with Google we receive your name, email address, and profile photo. This is the only way to create an account.
  • Your profile — the hunter name (handle) you choose and, optionally, a profile photo you upload.
  • Location — when you tap “check in”, we use your device’s precise location once, at that moment, to verify you are within 25 metres of the terrace. We do not track your location in the background or record your movements over time.
  • Gameplay — your check-ins (which terrace, when, points earned), crowns you hold, and leaderboard standings.
  • Notifications — if you turn on alerts, the push subscription for your device so we can notify you when someone steals your crown.
  • Technical — a session token stored on your device to keep you signed in, plus standard server logs (such as IP address and timestamps) used for security and abuse prevention.

2. How we use your data

  • To run the game: show shade near you, record check-ins, award points and crowns, and build the leaderboards.
  • To sign you in and keep your account secure.
  • To send the crown-steal alerts you have opted into.
  • To prevent cheating and abuse (e.g. faked locations) and keep the service reliable.

3. Legal basis (GDPR)

We process your data to provide the service you have asked for (performance of a contract), on the basis of your consent for location access and push notifications, and on our legitimate interest in keeping the game fair and secure. You can withdraw consent at any time.

4. Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal data. We use trusted service providers (“processors”) to operate Ombra:

  • Google — sign-in;
  • Vercel — website hosting;
  • Neon — database;
  • Ably — real-time updates;
  • a web-push service (via your browser vendor) to deliver notifications.

Your public profile — handle, avatar, and gameplay stats — is visible to other players on the leaderboards.

5. Retention

We keep your account and gameplay data for as long as your account is active. If you ask us to delete your account, we remove your personal data, except where we must keep limited records to comply with the law.

6. Your rights

Under the GDPR you can access, correct, delete, or export your data, object to or restrict processing, and withdraw consent. To exercise any of these, email selene.app.studio@outlook.com. You also have the right to complain to your local data protection authority (in Spain, the AEPD).

7. Cookies & storage

Ombra stores a session token in your browser’s local storage to keep you signed in. We do not use third-party advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

8. Children

Ombra is not directed at children under 16 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

9. Changes

We may update this policy; we’ll change the “last updated” date above and, for significant changes, let you know in the app.

10. Contact

Selene App Studio — selene.app.studio@outlook.com
